Reporting an Incident

When contacting the ICT Helpdesk please provide us with the below information.
Having contacted the ICT Helpdesk your incident or request will be logged, prioritised and assigned to an appropriate operative.

1

PC Workstation Name

There is shortcut on every college desktop with the workstation name or let us know if this is your own personal workstation
2

What is happening or not happening

Provide details of steps taken when the problem occurred and what you actually expected to happen
3

Screenshots or video

Screenshots or videos are very helpful to help us troubleshoot
4

Who is affected

Let us know who is affected: just you, or others around you
5

Your contact details

Let us know your student number, name and the best method to contact you
